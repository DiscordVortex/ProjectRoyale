LGBT Advocate Partnership

More information on LGBTAdvocates partnership with Vortex.

Posted by Lynx on February 19, 2018

Vortex has recently partnered with LGBTAdvocate to spread awareness about LGBT communtiy and STD/STI's.

What will happen and how will we work with LGBTAdvocate?

  • We will help spread awareness about STD and STI's.
  • Donations to charity.
  • Vortex will provide a support section managed by LGBTAdvocate on their discord for advice.
