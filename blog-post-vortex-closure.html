We're back!

More information on Royales closure vote.

Posted by Lynx on February 19, 2018

Royale recently had recently posted a vote to all of its members wether or not they wanted to see it close, due to in-active members luckily the poll had won 3-5. Royale's Chief Executive stated that if the poll wins there would be a lot of changed and things to ensure that Royale is active.

What type of changes?

  • Useless bots will be removed
  • Un-used channels will be deleted
  • The change-log will be moved to an online page to reduce spam
  • Announcements will be kept to a minimum.
  • Rules will be reviewed and edited to fit all age groups.
  • All staff will be demoted and new staff will be re-elected.
  • All giveaways have been suspended until some type of funding is achieved.
  • Royale will be partning with more servers
  • In-active members will be kicked and private messaged a re-join link.

    • More information to come in the upcoming weeks.
