Contributing

Vortex Project was founded and owned by Lynx, contributing to this project can involve becoming a consultant (3rd in command over Vortex) or becoming a department Director if your not the management person there is vacant helper roles.

If you want to give us your ideas or feedback please submit them here

Some tips for contributors

  • Director of Human Resources

  • Bot Development Team

FAQ

What happens after my application is submitted?

It will be reviewed by someone from our Human Resources department.

How long will it take?

We aim to review applications within 48 hours
